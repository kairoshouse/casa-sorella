The Firm

Our Identity

A specialised alternative for the modern resident who recognises that the integrity of their space is not the place to cut corners.

Casa Sorella Translates to Sister's House.

We treat your residence as we would our own.

In an industry of high-turnover platforms and unverified labour, Casa Sorella exists as a specialised alternative for the modern resident who recognises that the integrity of their space is not the place to cut corners.

We provide the efficiency of a quick-service visit backed by the technical authority of a licensed GC firm. Most projects are completed in under two hours, supported by a professional understanding of residential architecture.

Whether your walls are metal-stud, masonry, or traditional wood-frame, we verify the composition of the substrate before the first hole is drilled.
