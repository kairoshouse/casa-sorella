Our Identity

Casa Sorella translates to Sister's House.

We operate on a simple principle: we treat your residence with the technical care and protective discretion we would provide for our own. We are a specialized firm for those who prioritize time and space—offering a professionalized approach where competence is the baseline.

Casa Sorella  —  The Firm

Built for the Modern Resident.

CASA SORELLA provides quick-strike residential installations—TV mounting, art hanging, and furniture assembly—executed with the technical authority of a licensed general contracting firm.

We are a specialized firm for those who value their time and their space. Most of our projects are completed in under two hours, but that time is backed by a professional understanding of residential architecture. We know the composition of your walls—metal, wood, or masonry—ensuring every installation is permanent, level, and correct.
